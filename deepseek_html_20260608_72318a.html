02 — About

I make pictures that don’t shout. Slow observation, available light, and a quiet sense of composition. My work is an attempt to notice what’s already there — a glance, a shadow, a moment of stillness in movement.

I shoot on 35mm and digital, but the format matters less than the intention. Let the pictures do the talking. Keep it boring. That’s where the honesty lives.

Based between analogue and hard drives, I collaborate with individuals and brands who value patience over trends.
